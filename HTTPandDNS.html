HTTP and DNS

HTTP stands for HyperText Transfer Protocol and DNS stands for Domain Name System

-HTTP are the requests sent by computers over the internet in the form of packets. These requests are then sent to databases, DNS, and other destinationsto recieve information that is then sent back to your computer over the internet.

-DNS are a system of servers that keep track of IP addresses associated with each domain name. Domain names are .org, .com, .edu, and so on. When you send a request for a certain website or data, the DNS filters through the domain names and sends back the website or data to your computer so you can view what you requested.
